About the Game

Step into the exciting world of Study War, a fast-paced, quiz-based game inspired by Kahoot! Engage with friends or challenge yourself in dynamic rounds of trivia, all brought to life with vibrant visuals, flashy effects, and a smooth, intuitive UI. Every question feels thrilling with animations that make learning fun and competitive. Perfect for anyone who loves interactive quizzes, flashy presentation, and high-energy gameplay. Test your knowledge, compete for the top score, and enjoy a visually stunning experience that keeps players coming back for more!
